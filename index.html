Galaxy Growth: Game Manual

Core Concept: A cosmic idle simulator where players grow stars in "Garden Boxes" to earn energy and unlock rare celestial bodies.

1. The Gameplay Loop

  1. Buy Ingredients: Spend Energy to buy "Hydrogen Clusters".
  2. Place & Wait: Click a box. The gas spins for 10-30 seconds (Cooking time).
  3. The Result (RNG): The gas collapses into a Star.
  4. Passive Income: That star now generates Energy/sec forever (until you delete it).
  5. Upgrade: Use Energy to buy better gas types (Helium, Iron) or upgrade RNG Luck.

4. Longevity & The "End Game"

"How long will this game last?"

If we just let them build stars, they will get bored in 3 days. To make it last months, we introduce the "End Game Loop":

  • The Collection Book (Pokedex): Players want to find every star type (100+ variations). This takes weeks of RNG rolling.
  • Rebirth (Big Bang): Reset your galaxy for a permanent "Luck Multiplier". This extends gameplay infinitely.
  • Cosmic War (PvP): In the end game, players can build "Wormholes". If a neighbor is offline, you can siphon 5% of their gas. This forces players to log in daily to check defenses.
